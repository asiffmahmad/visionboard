## Full Description
*(Max 4000 characters — shown on the app detail page)*

```
Vision Board is your all-in-one personal productivity companion — 100% offline
and completely private. No cloud. No account. No data leaving your device.

──────────────────────────────────────────
  PLAN YOUR LIFE. TRACK YOUR PROGRESS.
──────────────────────────────────────────

🎯  VISIONS & GOALS
Create inspiring visions for every area of your life — career, health,
relationships, finance. Break each vision down into measurable goals with
target dates and track your progress step by step.

✅  TASK MANAGEMENT
Organise daily tasks under each goal. Mark them complete, set due dates, and
stay on top of what matters most today.

📖  JOURNAL
Write daily journal entries with a mood selector. Reflect on your wins,
challenges, and gratitude. Your thoughts stay on your device — always.

📝  NOTES
Capture ideas and notes instantly. Tag them to a goal or keep them free-form.
Full rich-text editing with search.

📊  HABIT TRACKER
Build positive habits with daily check-ins and a streak counter. Visual
progress rings keep you motivated.

📈  DASHBOARD
Get an at-a-glance summary of your open tasks, active habits, recent notes,
and upcoming goals — all in one beautiful screen.

──────────────────────────────────────────
  100% OFFLINE — YOUR DATA STAYS WITH YOU
──────────────────────────────────────────

Vision Board works without any internet connection. There is no sign-in,
no server, and no subscription. Everything is stored securely in a local
SQLite database on your device.

• No account required
• No personal data collected
• No ads, ever
• Export a full data backup at any time
• Restore from a backup with one tap

──────────────────────────────────────────
  SECURE BY DESIGN
──────────────────────────────────────────

• Local PIN / password authentication
• Optional biometric lock (fingerprint / face unlock)
• Credentials stored in Android Keystore via flutter_secure_storage
• No analytics SDK, no crash reporting, no third-party tracking

──────────────────────────────────────────
  BEAUTIFUL, MODERN DESIGN
──────────────────────────────────────────

Glassmorphism UI with smooth animations, dark mode, and Material 3 design
language. Vision Board feels premium from first launch.

Download Vision Board today and start turning your ideas into action.
```
